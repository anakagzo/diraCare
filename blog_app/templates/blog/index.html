Pregnancy 1 month
Antenatal care is the care you get from health professionals during your pregnancy It's sometimes called pregnancy care or maternity care. You'll be offered appointments with a midwife, or sometimes a doctor who specialises in pregnancy and birth (an obstetrician). You should start your antenatal care as soon as possible once you know you're pregnant. You can do this by contacting a midwife or GP, or referring yourself directly to maternity services near you.
